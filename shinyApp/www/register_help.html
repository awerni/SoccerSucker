Registration help

The betting game is just for fun. Don't take it to serious. It's money free and an alternative to professional betting. The winner will get 5 minutes of fame and probably a small prize.

First name

Use your first name or a nickname.

Surname

Use your surname.

Nationality

Use your country of origin rather than the adjective: Austria, Germany, Italy, Spain, Greece,...

Expert status

You are an expert, if you know at least three players of 3 different teams by name, if you understood the offside rule or frequently follow national or international soccer.

You can call yourself intermediate expert, if you follow soccer during world or european championships and have a basic understanding of the game. (default)

If you think, you are a beginner, you don't know much more than the number of players (11 per team) and the purpose of the game (to make more goals then the opponents).

Don't be too strict with yourself. Your expert status is never shown directly in the application. It is used for automated players (so called bots), to see, if experts perform better than beginners.
